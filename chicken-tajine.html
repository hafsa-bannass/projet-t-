Chicken Tajine

Chicken tajine, also known as Moroccan chicken tajine, is a popular North African dish made with chicken, vegetables, and a blend of spices. The dish is named after the earthenware pot in which it is traditionally cooked, called a tajine.

The chicken is typically seasoned with a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, and cinnamon. It is then cooked with onions, garlic, tomatoes, and various vegetables, such as carrots, potatoes, and zucchini. The dish is often finished with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, and served with couscous or bread.

Chicken tajine is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is enjoyed throughout North Africa and has become popular in many other parts of the world.

Prep time

20 min

Cook time

1-2 hours

Tags : Dinner

instructions

step 1

• Heat the olive oil in a large tajine or heavy-bottomed pot over medium-high heat.

step 2

• Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

step 3

• In the same pot, add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 3 minutes.

step 4

• Add the cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, and cayenne pepper to the pot and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

step 5

• Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine.

step 6

• Return the chicken to the pot, submerging it in the liquid. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 7

• Bring the liquid to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Simmer for 45-50 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.

step 8

• Add the green olives and preserved lemon slices to the pot.

step 9

• Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley and serve hot with moroccan bread.

ingredients

4 chicken thighs or drumsticks

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, diced

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1 teaspoon paprika

1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

2 cups chicken broth

1/2 cup green olives, pitted

1 preserved lemon, rinsed and sliced

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish
